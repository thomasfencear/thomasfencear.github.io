Thomas Fence — Northwest Arkansas's Fence Contractor

Thomas Fence (Paschal Enterprises, Inc.) has been installing fences in Northwest Arkansas since 1993 — over 30 years of service to homeowners, businesses, farms, and contractors throughout Benton and Washington Counties.

We're licensed under Arkansas Contractor's License No. 015501, which we've held continuously since 2005 and renew annually. We're also registered as a US Federal Contractor, AFA Certified, and CGAT-certified for gate automation — four credentials that most NWA fence companies don't carry.

Our crew is based in Springdale. We know the terrain, the soil, the local permit requirements, and the HOA standards across the NWA region. When you hire Thomas Fence, you're hiring a company that's been part of this community for decades.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Thomas Fence licensed in Arkansas?

Yes. Thomas Fence holds Arkansas Contractor License No. 015501-0427, maintained continuously since 2005 and renewed annually. You can verify this license through the Arkansas Contractors Licensing Board.

What areas of Arkansas does Thomas Fence serve?

Thomas Fence primarily serves Northwest Arkansas — Springdale, Bentonville, Rogers, Fayetteville, Bella Vista, Siloam Springs, and surrounding communities in Benton and Washington Counties. Contact us to confirm availability for your specific location.

Is Thomas Fence a federal contractor?

Yes. Thomas Fence holds an active US Federal Contractor Registration and is qualified to bid on government and commercial projects. This registration is maintained and renewed as required.

How long has Thomas Fence been in business?

Thomas Fence was founded in 1993. The company has been under current ownership since 2003, giving it over 30 years of continuous operation in Northwest Arkansas.

Does Thomas Fence require a deposit?

No deposit is required for standard installations. The exception is special order materials or custom metal work. Thomas Fence prefers to wait until the job is completed before requesting payment.

What types of fencing does Thomas Fence install?

Thomas Fence installs vinyl fence, wood fence, chain link fence, ornamental steel fence, farm fencing, and temporary fencing for residential, commercial, and agricultural properties. The company also installs estate gates and CGAT-certified automated gate systems.

Does Thomas Fence offer financing?

Yes. Financing is available through Hearth, a third-party lender. Customers can apply by scanning the QR code on their estimate or visiting the financing page.

What warranty does Thomas Fence offer?

Thomas Fence offers a one-year craftsmanship warranty included in every written contract. Ornamental steel panels also carry a lifetime residential warranty from the manufacturer.
