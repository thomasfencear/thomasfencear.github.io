Northwest Arkansas Fence Permits

Getting a permit is easy with our team. Thomas Fence isn't just a local contractor — we're your trusted partner in all things fencing.

Important Notes

Permit requirements can change. Always verify with your city or county before starting your project.

If your property is in an HOA, the HOA may have its own fence approval process separate from any city permit.

Thomas Fence does not pull permits for customers — each homeowner is responsible for obtaining their own permit. We are happy to help you understand the process.
