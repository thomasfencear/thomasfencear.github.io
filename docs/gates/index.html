Why CGAT Certification Matters

The CGAT (Certified Gate Automation Technician) designation is issued by the American Fence Association and requires demonstrated knowledge of gate operator mechanics, UL 325 safety standards, access control systems, and installation best practices.

It's the recognized industry credential for gate automation — and it matters because automated gates are heavy, fast-moving machines. An improperly installed gate operator without correct safety devices can cause serious injury. CGAT-certified installers know the required entrapment protection, force limits, and safety device placement for every gate type.
