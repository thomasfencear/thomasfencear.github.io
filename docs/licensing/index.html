Licensing & Credentials

Thomas Fence holds every license and certification your project requires — verified, current, and on file.

Licensed, Certified & Insured

Thomas Fence holds every credential your project requires.

Thomas Fence has been in business since 1993 and is bonded and insured. Greg and Kate Paschal have been the owners since May of 2003. From day one, their commitment has been to operate with full transparency — holding every license, certification, and registration required by state law and industry standards.

Thomas Fence holds an active Arkansas Contractor's License (No. 015501), maintained continuously since 2005 and renewed annually. This license is required by Arkansas state law for any fencing project exceeding $2,000, and you can verify our status at any time through the Arkansas Contractor's Licensing Board.

Through the American Fence Association and the Accredited Contractor Institute, Thomas Fence became Arkansas's first Certified Fence Contractor — an accreditation earned by demonstrating excellence in business practices, passing rigorous industry testing, and maintaining a trusted professional reputation. We renew every three years through advanced training and continuing education.

We are also a Certified Gate Automation Technician (CGAT) — the premier certification for automated gate installers — and a verified US Federal Contractor Registration (USFCR) vendor. Every job we take is covered by full liability insurance and a bonded crew, giving you complete confidence from the first post to the final inspection.

Our Credentials at a Glance

Every license and certification listed here is current, verifiable, and maintained in good standing.

Arkansas Contractor's License

License No. 015501 — Issued 2005, Renewed Annually

Required by Arkansas state law for any fencing project exceeding $2,000. Thomas Fence has held this license without interruption since 2005. You can verify our license status at any time through the Arkansas Contractor's Licensing Board.

AFA Certified Fence Contractor

Arkansas's First Certified Fence Contractor

Through the American Fence Association and the Accredited Contractor Institute (ACI), Thomas Fence became Arkansas's first Certified Fence Contractor. Awarded after proving excellence in business practices, passing industry-specific testing, and maintaining a trusted professional reputation. Renewed every three years through advanced training.

Certified Gate Automation Technician

CGAT — Premier Certification for Automated Gate Installers

The CGAT designation is awarded to technicians who have demonstrated exceptional experience, knowledge, safety awareness, and skills in the installation and servicing of automated gate systems. Thomas Fence holds this certification and maintains it through continuing education.

US Federal Contractor Registration

USFCR Verified Vendor

Thomas Fence is a verified US Federal Contractor Registration vendor, authorized to perform fencing contracts for government facilities, military installations, and large-scale public projects. This registration reflects strict federal compliance standards and qualifies Thomas Fence to serve projects beyond standard residential and commercial scope.

Bonded & Insured

Full Liability Coverage on Every Project

Thomas Fence is fully bonded and insured. Every project is covered from the first post to the final inspection, protecting your property and ensuring our crew operates under full liability coverage. Proof of insurance is available upon request.
