About Thomas Fence

Thomas Fence has been serving Northwest Arkansas since 1993. Headquartered at 653 Kawneer Dr in Springdale, we are a locally owned and operated fence company serving Bentonville and the surrounding NWA region. Greg Paschal has owned and operated Thomas Fence since 2003. We hold an Arkansas Contractor's License (No. 015501), US Federal Contractor Registration, AFA Certified Fence Contractor status, and CGAT Certification for gate automation.

No deposit is required for standard residential installations. We offer a one-year craftsmanship warranty on all work, in writing. Financing is available through Hearth — learn more about financing options.
