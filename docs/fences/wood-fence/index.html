Residential and Commercial Wood Fencing

Many Northwest Arkansas home and business owners choose wood fencing because it is versatile, natural, and beautiful. Wood fencing is customizable, blends with surroundings, and complements any landscaping style.

Wood fencing offers the highest level of customization, styles, colors, and options of any fencing material. Team up with the fence experts at Thomas Fence to find a quality wood fence at a reasonable price.

Wood Fence Key Features

Strong Posts

We use high-quality wood posts or steel PostMaster posts. PostMaster posts provide rot prevention and come with a limited lifetime warranty. They work with any wood fence style and eliminate the most common cause of wood fence failure.

Strong Wood Rails

Professional-grade rails perform better and last longer. The rails are the backbone of any wood fence — we use quality materials so your fence holds up to years of daily use, weather, and typical wear and tear.

High-Quality Wood Pickets

Thickness, width, and density all matter. We use quality pickets that are straight, properly dried, and free of defects that lead to warping and cupping over time.

Wood Fence Benefits

Naturally Beautiful

For many Northwest Arkansas homeowners, nothing can match the traditional beauty and strength of a custom wood fence. Wood blends naturally with landscaping and complements almost any architectural style.

Highly Customizable

Wood fencing is the most customizable fence type available. Height, style, finish, and color can all be tailored to your exact specifications. If a truly custom fence is what you want, choose our expert fence installers.

Strong and Durable

Cedar is naturally rot-resistant and well-suited for Northwest Arkansas weather. When installed with quality posts, rails, and hardware, a wood fence will last for many years with proper care.

Staining Your Wood Fence

Thomas Fence does not stain fences — fence staining requires a different skill set and we refer customers to trusted local staining companies. For timing: pressure-treated pine should wait 1–3 months before staining. Cedar can be stained immediately after installation.

Wood Fence FAQs

Do you install wood fences in Northwest Arkansas?

Yes. We install wood fences across the entire Northwest Arkansas region — Springdale, Bentonville, Rogers, Fayetteville, Bella Vista, Siloam Springs, and surrounding communities. Our crews are specially trained in wood fence installation and experienced with local soil conditions and weather patterns.

How long does it take before staining a new wood fence?

For pressure-treated pine, wait 1–3 months before staining so the wood can dry properly. Cedar can be stained immediately after installation. Thomas Fence does not provide staining services — we refer customers to trusted local staining companies.

What is the best wood fence type for Northwest Arkansas?

Cedar is naturally rot-resistant and performs exceptionally well in Northwest Arkansas weather. It is our most popular wood choice. Pressure-treated pine is also a durable, cost-effective option. Our team can help you choose the right wood species and style for your budget, property, and goals. Read more in our blog post: Not All Cedar Fences Are Created Equal.

Does Thomas Fence require a deposit?

No deposit is required for standard installations. The exception is special order materials or custom work. Thomas Fence prefers to wait until the job is completed before requesting payment.

Do I need a permit for a wood fence?

It depends on where you live. Some cities in Northwest Arkansas require fence permits; others do not. Visit our Fence Permits page for a full list of cities that require permits with links to each city's permitting portal. Thomas Fence does not pull permits — each customer is responsible for obtaining their own.
