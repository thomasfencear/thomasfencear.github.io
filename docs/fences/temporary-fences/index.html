Temporary Fence FAQs

Do I need a permit for a temporary fence in Arkansas?

Permit requirements for temporary fencing vary by city and project type. Many short-term temporary fence installations do not require a permit, but construction sites and longer-term installations may. Thomas Fence can help you understand what applies to your project — permit responsibility ultimately rests with the property owner or contractor of record.

What types of temporary fencing does Thomas Fence install?

Thomas Fence installs freestanding temporary panel fencing and temporary chain link fencing. Panel fencing is quick to install and requires no digging. Temporary chain link is suited for larger perimeters and longer project durations where a more secure barrier is needed.

How quickly can Thomas Fence install temporary fencing?

Temporary fencing can typically be installed faster than permanent fencing since no concrete or deep footings are required for panel systems. Contact us at (479) 636-4732 with your timeline and we will do our best to accommodate your project schedule.

Does Thomas Fence handle removal when the project is done?

Yes. When your project is complete, call us to schedule removal and pickup. We handle both installation and removal as part of the temporary fencing service.

Is a deposit required for temporary fencing?

Deposit requirements for temporary fencing projects are determined on a per-project basis. Your estimator will confirm payment terms when reviewing your project scope and timeline.

Does Thomas Fence serve commercial and government job sites?

Yes. Thomas Fence is a registered US Federal Contractor and holds Arkansas Contractor License No. 015501. We are qualified to serve commercial, industrial, and government job sites requiring temporary fencing across Northwest Arkansas.
