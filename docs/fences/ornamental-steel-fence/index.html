Residential and Commercial Ornamental Steel Fencing

Ornamental steel fences are an attractive, durable choice for residential or commercial property owners. Whatever your requirements, Thomas Fence will find the right fence for your property. Strength and security are premium features of ornamental steel fences — and the elegant appearance elevates any home or business.

Ornamental steel fencing is the top choice for higher-end homes, high-traffic areas that require an ultra-strong fence, or premium decorative accents when nothing but the best will do.

Ornamental Steel Fencing Key Features

Strong Ornamental Steel Posts

Our ornamental steel posts are available in a range of gauges to provide the optimal level of strength for your specific application — residential, commercial, or industrial.

Premium Rails

Heavy-gauge steel rails provide the structural backbone of your ornamental fence. We size rail gauge to the demand of the application so your fence stays true for years.

Premium Pickets with ProFusion Welding

Our pickets are manufactured with ProFusion welding technology, which hides the hardware and creates a strong, sleek, finished look that elevates the appearance of any property.

Ornamental Steel Fence FAQs

Are ornamental steel fences available in different heights?

Yes. Ornamental steel fences are available in various heights, depending on your needs. We can install residential ornamental fences as low as 3 feet for decorative purposes or taller heights for security applications. Our team will help you choose the right height for your property and local code requirements.

Are ornamental steel fences a good choice for pool enclosures?

Yes. Ornamental steel fences are an excellent choice for pool enclosures, since they are resistant to rust and corrosion from pool chemicals and moisture. They also provide the open, elegant look that many homeowners prefer around pools while meeting pool safety code requirements with self-closing, self-latching gates.

Do ornamental steel fences require maintenance?

Very little. The powder-coated finish on ornamental steel fences prevents rust, corrosion, and deterioration. Unlike wood fences, there is no painting, staining, or sealing required. An occasional rinse with a garden hose is typically all that's needed to keep your ornamental steel fence looking great.

Does Thomas Fence require a deposit for ornamental steel fence installations?

A deposit may be required for custom metal work or special orders. Standard ornamental steel fence installations do not require a deposit. Thomas Fence prefers to wait until the job is completed before requesting payment.

Can ornamental steel fences be automated?

Yes. Ornamental steel gates can be equipped with automated gate operators. Thomas Fence is CGAT (Certified Gate Automation Technician) certified, so we handle both the fence installation and gate automation as a complete package. Learn more on our Gate Automation page.
