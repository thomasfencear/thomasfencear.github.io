Residential and Commercial Vinyl Fencing

Vinyl fencing is popular due to its low-maintenance, eco-friendly features. Vinyl fencing is an attractive option suitable for homes and businesses across Northwest Arkansas.

Truly unique in the benefits it provides, vinyl fencing is an attractive addition to any residential and commercial property. No other fence material comes close to matching the clean, fresh look of vinyl fencing while simultaneously providing an expansive selection of styles and colors.

Although vinyl privacy fencing tends to be the most popular choice among property owners, there are other styles and choices available. From vinyl rail fences to vinyl picket fences to semi-privacy and even more, each style has its own benefits. Whatever your preference and need may be, you will see that vinyl fencing can meet it. Eco-friendly, maintenance-free, and attractive, vinyl fencing is an excellent choice for property owners across Northwest Arkansas.

Vinyl Fencing Key Features

Each component of vinyl fencing works together to create a stunning, low-maintenance fence.

Strong Vinyl Posts

Quality matters. Our professional-grade vinyl fence posts will remain strong and last for years when properly installed by our expert fence installers. Engineered to withstand Northwest Arkansas weather, our vinyl fences are designed, built, and installed to look great while lasting for decades.

Strong Vinyl Rails

We only use high-grade vinyl rails, which sets us apart from many of our competitors. The weakest part of a lower-quality vinyl fence is the horizontal rails. We install professional-grade vinyl rails, ensuring that your fence is strong enough to withstand the demands placed upon it.

High-Quality Vinyl Pickets

Our vinyl fence pickets are also high-quality, just like our posts and rails. While thicker vinyl walls will cost a bit more than our competitors' options, we believe in installing quality fencing that endures. Using high-quality vinyl fencing is essential if you want your fence to stand the test of time.

Vinyl Fencing Advantages

Property owners across Northwest Arkansas often choose vinyl fencing for the following reasons.

Vinyl Fencing is Attractive

Available in a wide selection of colors, patterns, and styles, our vinyl fences are an attractive addition to any property. We even offer vinyl fencing in wood grain textures, meaning you get the look of wood, with the benefit of long-lasting vinyl. Our vinyl fences are available in a variety of styles and heights. Complement your existing property with a full-privacy fence, a semi-privacy fence, a picket fence, or a rail fence that matches the look and feel of your home or business.

Vinyl Fencing is a Great Value

Depending on the size, style, and requirements of your fence project, vinyl fencing in Northwest Arkansas can be a cost-effective choice. While the initial cost may be more expensive than wood or chain link fencing, vinyl fencing may still be a better choice, because there are no ongoing maintenance costs after installation.

Vinyl Fencing is Low Maintenance

Vinyl fencing is fade resistant and will not fracture, rot, peel, or crack. Characterized by its low-maintenance features, vinyl fencing will endure without the need for heavy-duty cleaning. If it does need to be cleaned, it can be rinsed using a garden hose or washed with a gentle cleanser and water.

Vinyl Fence FAQs

Do you install vinyl fences in Northwest Arkansas?

Yes. We are a leading vinyl fencing contractor across the Northwest Arkansas region. We specially train our crews in the latest techniques for installing vinyl fences. Our experienced installers ensure that your fence will be installed correctly so that it functions properly, holds up to the weather, and looks beautiful while protecting what matters most to you.

Is a vinyl fence a good choice for the Northwest Arkansas area?

If you want a low-maintenance, clean, fresh, durable, strong fence, then vinyl fencing is an excellent choice. Vinyl fencing is manufactured to withstand the climate and weather conditions of the region and is designed to last for decades. Vinyl fencing has risen in popularity because it is easy to care for and requires little attention, making it a less expensive option over time.

How durable are vinyl fences?

Vinyl fencing is designed to last for decades and is extremely durable when installed properly. It is one of the more eco-friendly alternatives available since it does not need to be painted or treated with chemicals, it can be recycled when it is no longer needed, and it does not need to be replaced often, thereby reducing the number of new resources needed.

What materials make up a vinyl fence?

Most vinyl fencing is created with polyvinyl chloride, or PVC, which is the world's third most manufactured synthetic plastic. PVC is a material widely used in many other industries and is well-suited for long-lasting fence applications.

Can a vinyl fence be painted?

Vinyl fencing is made from nonporous PVC, causing it to be resistant to paint. Many Northwest Arkansas residents select vinyl specifically because it does not need to be painted, stained, or sealed. Unlike wood fencing, vinyl fencing retains its original color and shine for the duration of its installation. Although vinyl fences cannot be painted, there is a wide variety of colors available.

Does Thomas Fence require a deposit?

No deposit is required for standard installations. The exception is special order materials or custom work. Thomas Fence prefers to wait until the job is completed before requesting payment.
