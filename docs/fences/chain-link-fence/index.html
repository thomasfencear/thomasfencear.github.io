Residential and Commercial Chain Link Fencing

Chain link fencing is a dependable and reliable fencing option for budget-conscious property owners across Northwest Arkansas. When you need to reliably secure large areas at a reasonable price, chain link fencing is the perfect solution.

No other type of fencing can match chain link fencing's strength, longevity, and low-maintenance requirements at the same price point. Chain link fencing is the definition of strength, durability, and dependability.

Chain Link Fence Options

We offer three chain link fence options to meet different needs and budgets.

Traditional / Galvanized Chain Link

The classic choice. Galvanized steel wire provides maximum durability and a clean, industrial look. Ideal for securing large properties, construction sites, and utility areas.

PVC and Polymer Coated Chain Link

Vinyl-coated chain link adds color and an extra layer of corrosion protection. Available in black, green, and other colors to blend with your landscape or meet HOA guidelines.

Privacy Slat Chain Link

Add privacy to any chain link fence by inserting slats through the chain link fabric. Available in multiple colors, privacy slats are a cost-effective way to add screening to an existing or new chain link fence.

Chain Link Fencing Top Benefits

Economical

Chain link is typically the least expensive fence type, making it the go-to choice when budget is the primary consideration.

Secure

Chain link provides a strong physical barrier that is difficult to cut through or climb. Ideal for securing pools, yards, commercial lots, and utility areas.

Strong and Low-Maintenance

Galvanized chain link resists rust and corrosion. Once installed, it requires virtually no maintenance for years.

Chain Link Fence FAQs

Do you install chain link fences in Northwest Arkansas?

Yes. We are a trusted chain link fencing contractor across the entire Northwest Arkansas region — Springdale, Bentonville, Rogers, Fayetteville, Bella Vista, Siloam Springs, and surrounding communities. Our crews are trained in all types of chain link installation, from residential yards to large commercial enclosures.

Is chain link fence a good choice for residential properties?

Yes. Chain link fences are an excellent choice for a wide array of residential situations. While many homeowners choose basic chain link fencing for its lower cost, chain link also offers incredible durability, surprising beauty with color coating options, and a multitude of privacy and security features with privacy slats and other add-ons.

Can chain link fence be used for pool enclosures?

Yes. Chain link is a practical and code-compliant pool fence option. We can install self-closing, self-latching gates to meet pool safety requirements. Vinyl-coated chain link is a popular choice for pools as it resists the moisture and chemical exposure near pool areas.

Does Thomas Fence require a deposit?

No deposit is required for standard installations. Thomas Fence prefers to wait until the job is completed before requesting payment.

Do I need a permit for a chain link fence?

It depends on your city. Visit our Fence Permits page for a list of Northwest Arkansas municipalities that require permits, with links to each city's permitting portal. Thomas Fence does not pull permits — each customer is responsible for obtaining their own.
