Residential Fence FAQs

How much will a new residential fence cost?

Fence prices are dependent upon the type of material and style you choose. We provide quotes for free. Simply reach out to our team through our online form or give us a call and our friendly, experienced staff will explain the process, schedule a consultation, and provide you with an accurate quote.

Important: before focusing solely on price, we strongly recommend you contact one of our fence experts. There are cheaper, low-quality fence materials available that will not last. Fences must stand up to Northwest Arkansas's high winds, harsh weather, and typical wear and tear. Shopping for the cheapest fence is usually not the best way to decide which fence is best to protect your home and the people you care about.

How long will it take to get my fence installed?

Typically, it only takes a few days for one of our residential fence experts to walk your property and provide a detailed estimate. Once you approve the estimate, we can often install residential fences within 1 to 3 weeks. A project manager will call 1 to 2 days before crews arrive. Special-order materials or custom options may add additional lead time — we will inform you in advance.

Do I need a permit for a residential fence?

It depends on where you live. Some cities in Northwest Arkansas require fence permits; others do not. Visit our Fence Permits page for a full list of cities with permit requirements and links to each city's permitting portal. Also note that city permits differ from HOA/POA requirements — check with your HOA if applicable. Thomas Fence does not pull permits; each customer is responsible for obtaining their own.

Do you offer a warranty for residential fences?

Absolutely. Thomas Fence offers a 1-year workmanship warranty on all fence installations. We only use professional-grade materials from reputable fence manufacturers, which means you will also be covered by the manufacturer's product warranty. Your coverage will depend on the type of product you purchase.

Does Thomas Fence require a deposit?

No deposit is required for standard installations. The exception is special order materials or custom metal work. Thomas Fence prefers to wait until the job is completed before requesting payment.

Does Thomas Fence offer financing?

Yes. Financing is available through Hearth, a third-party lender serving the home improvement industry. Customers can apply online at thomasfencear.com/financing/ or ask about the QR code on your estimate. Loans up to $250,000 with no impact to your credit score to check your rate.
